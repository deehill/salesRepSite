the files are intendd to provide an overview of the 
 different marketing/landing pages that are envisioned 
 as being needed for the JNI book process

 Goal of JNIBooks is to create a system to provide cheap/cheapest 
	textbooks to tgt college users
	Major components of the system will consist of:
		also have advertisers
		collegeUser -tgt college users
		SaasPartner -(SAAS partners) - provide Saas apps/partners for Education
		LocalBiz	-local/community biz looking to advertise to college users
		BookStore	-Actual bookstore process to provide books to college users
		rewardsDepot-Process for the users who refer other users/viral growth process
		salesRep	-For the user/student that takes on the SalesRep role to pull in Ad Rev
		*adminInternal
		*monitorInternal

 JNI expects that each of these "components" will have its own landing/marketing 
	page to "explain" the process to the tgt user/audience

The files in the repos are targeted to the userLandingPage process:
	(files for the other associated site/landingPages haven't been developed
	 the idea is to create the basic userLandingPage, and use that as a template 
	 to develop the rest of the landingPageWebApps
	 -The basic structure/framework will be similar)
